About Us

The Establishment of Eden's Cafe`

In 2016, Muhammad Arya Adi Darma, the founder, gathered six of his friends and proposed to create a cafe that focused instead on coffee, he wanted a place that focused on selling desserts.

In 2020, the Covid-19 Pandemic forced Arya to close his cafe, then Arya asked Fathi, Head of Marketing, to find a way to continue selling Shaved Ice in the midst of the Covid-19 Pandemic, then this website appeared.

After 3 years of waiting and restarting his business from scratch. In 2023, Arya's business has soared rapidly and succeeded in opening 85 branches throughout Indonesia.

Now, Arya is still busy serving customers with his six friends at the main Eden's Cafe on Jalan Batu Benawa, Banjarmasin.
